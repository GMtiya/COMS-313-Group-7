Personal Safety Tips

  • Download 'Life360' and share it with trustworthy friends.
  • Walk in well-lit areas whenever possible.
  • Keep emergency contact numbers saved on your phone.
  • Do not share personal information with strangers.
  • Report suspicious behaviour to campus protection services.
  • Prioritise walking in groups at all costs.

GBVF Awareness

Students should know where to find support and how to report gender-based violence safely and confidentially

Online Safety

Use strong passwords and avoid using the same passwords for different logins. Avoid suspicious links and protect your personal information online.
